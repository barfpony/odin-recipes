Chicken Tikka Masala

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 2 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 2 tsp + 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 3 tsp + 2 tbsp salt
  • 2 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp minced fresh ginger
  • 2 serrano peppers finely chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, chopped
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 can tomato sauce (8 oz)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 or 3 chicken breasts (cut into pieces)
  • 2 tsp chicken stock
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • Potatos

Steps

  1. Marinate Chicken: In a large bowl, combine yogurt, 1 tablespoon lemon juice, 2 teaspoons cumin, 1 teaspoon cinnamon, 2 teaspoons cayenne, 2 teaspoons black pepper, teaspoon ginger, and 3 teaspoons salt. Stir in chicken, cover, and refrigerate for 1 hour.
  2. Engage the Oven (difficulty: hard): Set oven to high broil
  3. Broil the Chicky Bois: Place chicken pieces on broiler pan. Cook until chicken reaches 165 (roughly 12 minutes).
  4. Saucy Sauce: Melt butter in a large heavy skillet over medium heat. Saute garlic and peppers for 1 minute. Season with 2 teaspoons cumin, 2 teaspoons paprika, and 2 teaspoons salt. Stir in tomato sauce and cream. Simmer on low heat until sauce thickens, about 20 minutes. Add grilled chicken and simmer for 10 minutes. Transfer to a serving platter, and garnish with fresh cilantro.
